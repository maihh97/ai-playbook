Patients are admitted through emergency departments or referrals.
Clinical teams review patient data and make informed decisions.
Electronic health records store patient information securely.
Healthcare systems must ensure safety, privacy, and efficiency.

The doctor assesses the patient and orders diagnostic tests.
Test results are reviewed and treatment plans are created.
Nurses monitor patients and administer medications.
Follow-up appointments are scheduled after discharge.

Healthcare professionals follow clinical guidelines and policies.
